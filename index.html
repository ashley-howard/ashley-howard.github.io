What I do

Web design & development

Your website, beautifully responsive, and looks great even on mobiles. People like to check websites on the move, so content needs to be displayed correctly on their devices. Everything works how you expect it to. More on my services.

CMSs

Content management systems. What are they? They enable you to take control of your website easily, even when you're on the go. WordPress is one of the most popular CMSs. You'll be able to add, edit, and delete content at ease. Don't worry if you've never used one before, when I've finished your website I'll show you how to use it. More on my services.

E-commerce

In 2019, e-retail sales accounted for 14.1% of all retail sales worldwide. This figure is expected to reach 22% in 2023 (Statista). So it's no wonder many businesses are moving/extending their business online. Many people love buying things from the comfort of their home, and it should be a rather simple process. That's why I like to use Shopify. It's great for beginners, and I can teach you how to use it if need be. What's more, Shopify has many free tutorials for you to continue learning, whether it be how to use the site, or how to increase sales or reach.

COVID-19: While businesses everywhere are taking precautions to make sure everyone stays safe, creating an online store helps you to continue your sales, even without a physical store front.

Analytics

Why end there? I can add Google Analytics to your website so you can see information about your visitors or customers in real time. You'll know more about their location, age, much more configurable data. Read more over at Google Analytics.
