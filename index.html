I build with accessibility in mind

Have you ever looked at a website and squinted at the text? Maybe the font was too small, or maybe the colour of the text was too similar to the background. I’m here to prevent that from happening.

  • I create accessible styles

    I’ll help you pick out the best colours and fonts that not only suit your company’s branding, but are also legible and contrast well.

  • I use semantic and SEO-friendly code

    Google prioritises websites whose code is easily readable and uses modern practises. I’ll help you with that.
