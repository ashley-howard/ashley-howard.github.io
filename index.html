What I do

Web design & development

I design and develop modern, clean, and mobile-friendly bespoke websites for small businesses and individuals, from scratch, to fit your needs. I also work with businesses who have been affected by COVID and help them grow their business by creating a price plan that works for them.

At the moment I only offer completely bespoke services for static sites, Wordpress, and certain frameworks. Get in touch to find out if I'm the right fit for your project.

If you're interested in working with me, feel free to submit a free quote. You can also read more on my services.

CMSs (e.g. Wordpress)

Content management systems. What are they? They enable you to take control of your website easily, even when you're on the go. WordPress is one of the most popular CMSs. You'll be able to add, edit, and delete content at ease. Don't worry if you've never used one before, when I've finished your website I'll show you how to use it. More on my services.

E-commerce (e.g. WooCommerce)

In 2019, e-retail sales accounted for 14.1% of all retail sales worldwide. This figure is expected to reach 22% in 2023 (Statista). So it's no wonder many businesses are moving/extending their business online. Many people love buying things from the comfort of their home, and it should be a rather simple process. That's why I'd recommend using either WooCommerce or Shopify. They both have great tools and online resources to help you continue learning, whether it be how to use the site, or how to increase sales or reach, and I can help you at the start if need be.

COVID-19: While businesses everywhere are taking precautions to make sure everyone stays safe, creating an online store helps you to continue your sales, even without a physical store front.

Aftercare & Maintenance

Maintaining a website can be a challenge or even time-consuming, that's why I offer extra services bespoke for your needs. I have PAYG (Pay As You Go) and Pay Monthly options so you can choose the best fit for you. Only need something updated once a month? My PAYG plan is great for that. Or do you need me to have control of many aspects of your website, or regularly update things? You may be better suited to my Pay Monthly option.

Or do you already have a website and need just some extra help? I can help you get started or help you with any problems you may be having.

More on my aftercare.

What I use

Modern technologies

I always keep up-to-date with the latest trends and documentation that HTML, CSS, and JavaScript have to offer. I also build websites from scratch, to give you everything you need, no more no less. Your website isn't weighed down by tools you don't need. This means your website will be light and load super fast. I also code semantically, which means browsers can better understand your content (and also easier to read for humans).

If you'd like something with a bit more functionality and ease of use, I'd recommend using a service like Wordpress or Shopify, or even a web builder may fit your needs.

So whether you’re a blogger, small business or something a little bigger, I’m here to get you up and running. If need something in particular, let's chat about it.

Analytics

Why end there? I can add Google Analytics to your website so you can see information about your visitors or customers in real time. You'll know more about their location, age, much more configurable data. Read more over at Google Analytics.

Beautiful high-res media

Tight budget? Why not take a look at some royalty-free options, such as Unsplash and Pexels which have got you sorted with over 1,000,000 HD photos and videos to jazz up your website at a low low price of free. You’d be surprised how much of a difference a few images make.
